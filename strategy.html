SCHEDULE OF STRATEGY

  1. Publicity and Event organization (100 points)

    2. The Publicity is measure by visibility of paraphernalia as well as audience turn-out. Each department publicizes the event through whatever publicity material they wish to use, for example, banners, posters, flyers, street plays and other forms of campaigns during lunch hours. They need to ensure that they have a maximum strength audience for stage and theatre events. Class teachers must ensure that their entire class participates in the right event/s in consultation with the event coordinators. The format provided should ensure that event coordinators get all the required information from each class to collate all data .Attendance of each registered student must be accounted for to ensure full participation. Theme for promoting Blossoms is: Evolutions of Art forms.

    The dean, HODs and teachers must ensure that there is 25% participation of students from their team/deanery in each of the four categories (Art, Lit, Stage & Theatre). This can be done by allotting a team of teachers to co-ordinate between departments and classes, to ensure equal distribution among events categories.

  2. Certification (10 points)

    3. Blossoms Certification Day for the Department must be ensured.

    The writing/signing of certificates and announcement of results on the deanery Certification day must be organized. Importance is also given to how the certification ceremony is made a special occasion for all students to receive the certificates from the respective event co-coordinators.

  3. Participation and Documentation (150 Points)

    4. Every department teams are requested to maintain records like participant per event, registration details, absentees list, result sheets, judges lists etc which will be checked by the SWO office at the Intra level evaluation time.100 Percent participation is expected from each department team.
