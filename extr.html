INTER DEANERY DANCE EXTRAVAGANZA

Event will be held on the day of IN-BLOOM, on 8 and 9 of Feb 2019

Every team Participating will be given grades and points (Based on the performance)

Minimum number of teams from a deanery: 4

Maximum Number of teams per deanery: 8

Minimum number of dancers in a team: 25 and no maximum limit

Minimum time limit per team: 7 minutes

Maximum time limit per team: 8 minutes

Maximum number of songs per team: 3

Maximum number of members in a team and maximum audience support for the event is the most important criteria for grading.

Instruments, props and costume usage are optional and concentration is given on the dance to be performed by the deanery/team. The team needs to bear expenses of any costumes used and the deanery does not take in charge of the same.

Songs should be from Indian Movies/folk songs.

The registration details which include the names and register numbers of the team members and the selected song must be submitted to the SWO on or before 21 January, 2018 by the team leader.

If there exists repetition of songs between any two teams, the team which comes first with the song would be given preference and the other team will be asked to change their song.

Venues will be intimated to the teachers in charge once it is scheduled and will be allotted to the deaneries by the SWO
