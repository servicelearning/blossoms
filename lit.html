JAM

JAM

  • JAM master decides rounds.
  • 8-9 participants are panelists who bang on the table if an error is committed, list out the error and speak if the JAM master agrees with the person.
  • +5 for correct correction, -5 for wrong correction, +10 for every 5 seconds the participant speaks.
  • Additional points for getting the buzzer at the end of the minute. No participant is eliminated during the course of JAM.
  • Judged on clarity, content, presentation, relevance to the topic & language.
  • Participants lose points on late start, early start, speech defect, grammatical error.
  • Time: 2 hour
  • Top 2 teams will be selected for finals on 8 to 9 Feb, 2019

    • Thursday, 13th of December 2018
    3rd Block, 2nd Floor, room no. 329
