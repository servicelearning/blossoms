GENERAL GUIDELINES

INTRA DEPARTMENT COMPETITION

General Guidelines:

Department should ensure that all their students participate in minimum one of the 17 events.

The Students are allowed to participate in as many as events that they are interested to participate depending on the schedule and timings of the events.

In this edition of Blossoms, the Faculty of Engineering will be divided into 3 teams from the deaneries. The teams are as follows:

  1. Science & Humanities
  2. Non Circuit (Mechanical, Civil & Architecture)
  3. Circuit (CS/IT, EC, & EEE)

The events are divided into 4 categories for this edition of Blossoms

  1. ART Events
  2. LITERARY Events
  3. STAGE Events
  4. THEATRE Events

Department event coordinators should ensure that, there should be equal participation in every event. Class teachers would play a key role in ensuring that all students of their class participate in at least one relevant event. To ensure this, a format has been provided along with a demo page. Students absent for their assigned event must participate any of the other following events to ensure 100% participation from the class.

The total point for BLOSSOMS at intra level is 400.

Art & Literary events carry 5 points each -> 10 x 5 = 50 Points

Stage Events carry 10 points each -> 5 x 10 = 50 Points

Theater Events carry 20 points each - > 2 x 20 = 40 Points

260 points will be allotted by SWO for Strategy, certification, & Participation

To ensure recognition, all students will be entitled for the participation certificates.
